Privacy Policy

Transparency and user trust are core principles of Stacey Development and Joker Gems.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Joker Gems handles user data.

The application is designed with a strong focus on simplicity, privacy, and transparency.

No Data Collection

Joker Gems does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

- Name, email, or contact data
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Usage analytics

No Data Storage

Since no data is collected, no user information is stored on any servers or databases.

All interactions with the application remain fully local to the user’s device.

No Data Sharing

No information is shared with third parties.

The application does not include any integrations with advertising networks, analytics providers, or external tracking systems.

Third-Party Services

Joker Gems does not use any third-party SDKs or services that collect user data.

This ensures a clean and safe experience without hidden tracking mechanisms.

User Rights

Because no data is collected or processed, there is no personal information stored or managed by the developer.

This eliminates risks related to data misuse or unauthorized access.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if application functionality changes.

Any updates will be clearly reflected on this page.
